The Game as Chess
I interpret VALORANT as a real-time adaptation of chess: controlling space, trading resources efficiently, and leveraging information to anticipate and counter enemy decision-making.
Psychological Pillar
Players operate under significant cognitive load and emotional volatility, both of which directly impact performance and decision-making. A coach must provide psychological structure.
Discipline (In & Out)
Performance starts from life outside the game. Discipline is important, but it doesn't need to be overly strict; ideally, it fits the personality and the team. Balance is key.
Core Fundamentals
For me, the core of a team is discipline, communication, and mutual support. A strong team built on players who help each other grow, stay motivated, and constantly strive to improve.
