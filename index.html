IGL & COMMUNICATION

We believe in the player's own perception. We can all be IGL in different rounds, as in VALORANT there are many aspects and situations given their direct play and utility.

"What the IGL says" is a concept that should be largely abandoned. It is a game with infinite variables that requires the attention and active action of all members of the team. Roles are constantly changing; you should not limit yourself to making an aggressive play because you are the smoker, or avoid lurking because you are the entry. Be flexible.
